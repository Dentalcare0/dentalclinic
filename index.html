What is Dental care?

Dental care refers to the practice of maintaining the health of your teeth, gums, and mouth through proper hygiene, regular checkups, and professional treatments. It involves both preventive care—to stop dental problems before they start—and corrective care, which addresses existing issues like cavities, gum disease, or damaged teeth.
